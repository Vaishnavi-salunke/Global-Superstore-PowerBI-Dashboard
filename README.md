## Overview

This project presents a comprehensive Business Intelligence dashboard developed in Power BI using the Global Superstore dataset. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, enabling analysis of revenue, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate data visualization, business analytics, KPI tracking, and decision-making capabilities through an interactive dashboard experience.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall business performance using key metrics.
* Identify high-performing products and customers.
* Evaluate sales and profit trends across regions and markets.
* Monitor category and sub-category performance.
* Support strategic decision-making through data-driven insights.

---

## Dashboard Structure

### Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of business performance through:

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Orders
* Total Quantity Sold
* Average Discount
* Profit Margin

---

### Sales Analysis

Focused on sales performance across different business dimensions:

* Sales by Region
* Sales by Market
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Category Performance Analysis

---

### Product & Customer Insights

Provides detailed analysis of business drivers:

* Top Products by Sales
* Top Customers by Sales
* Profit by Sub-Category
* Segment-wise Sales Analysis

---

## Key Business Insights

### Sales Performance

* Generated over 13M in total sales.
* Strong sales growth observed across the analyzed period.
* Regional performance varies significantly across markets.

### Profitability

* Technology category generated the highest profit.
* Office Supplies contributed consistent profitability.
* Certain products generated losses despite strong sales volume.

### Customer Insights

* Consumer segment contributed the highest share of sales.
* A small group of customers accounted for a significant portion of revenue.

### Product Insights

* High-performing technology products drove overall revenue growth.
* Profitability varied considerably among sub-categories.
